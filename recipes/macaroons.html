Macaroon Recipe

Description

Fantabulous colored, bite-sized macaroons. Divide batter into thirds and color one-third pink and flavor with raspberry flavoring; color one-third yellow and flavor with lemon flavoring; color one-third pale blue and flavor with vanilla. Fill and make sandwich cookies with filling of choice, like peach jam or strawberry preserves.

Ingredients

  • 3 Egg Whites
  • 6 Tbsp Castor Sugar
  • 1 Cup Finely Ground Almonds
  • 1.5 Tsp Finely Ground Almonds
  • 1.3 Cups Confectioners' Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 320 degrees F. Line Baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Whisk egg whites until thick; whisk castor sugar into egg whites until glossy and holds stiff peaks.
  3. Place sieve over bowl and sieve both almond meal and confectioners' sugar into the egg white mixture. Gently folding to retain as much air as possible.
  4. Separate the meringue into separate bowls to flavor and color. Spoon into piping bag with a 3/8 in tip and pipe 1 inch disks onto the baking sheet.
  5. Let cookies stand at room temperature for about 15 minutes. Pick up and drop the baking sheet from several inches to adhere cookies to the backing sheet.
  6. Bake in preheated over until tops are dry; let cool completely before peeling them off.
